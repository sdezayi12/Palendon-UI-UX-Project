Welcome to Palendon!

Your gateway to celebrating Palestine in London. Whether you're keen on trying dishes at local restaurants or immersing in the vibrant scenes of live music and galleries, we've got you covered. Our standout feature is the Calculator, which ensures that planning your outings is as seamless as it is fun. By selecting your interests and providing some details like your event choice, ticket preferences, desired menu items or transport method - we deliver a breakdown of all associated costs before you have even stepped out the door. Click on the 'Calculator' link on the sidebar to start planning your adventure today!
